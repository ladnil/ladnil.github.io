Hello.

I’m Linda, a developing developer fulfilling my fill for full-stack. I’m a digital marketer, rookie coder, data (actually, all-round) nerd and design enthusiast that enjoys making websites look and work better for everyone that uses it. I believe in setting goals and standards, meeting them, and raising them again.

With a weird, winding and wonderful work background, I thrive when allowed to flex my left and right brain tendencies to deliver creative and analytical solutions that are elegantly simple, but most importantly: effective. I love to learn and I’m driven by a desire to understand, do and do better, in all aspects of my life, and since my first taste of coding I’m hooked. Moreover, it has been extremely gratifying to see how my previous studies and work background can be applied to my new field of study. I’m excited and eager to further immerse myself in the tech community and different programming languages.

I draw inspiration and wisdom from my experiences with the world and people around me, and aspire to work with and for people and organisations committed to getting more girls and women into STEM, areas particularly close to my heart. I firmly believe in giving back, sharing knowledge and inclusivity, and hope to inspire others to follow a similar path into tech.

A good day is when I've achieved a lot and made people happy in the process, letting the results speak for itself.

Me in a nutshell. I hope you enjoy viewing my portfolio and feel free to get in touch or find out more about me via the links below.
